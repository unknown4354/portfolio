About Me

My Journey & Expertise

With over 7 years of experience in web development, I've worked with startups and enterprises to build scalable and user-friendly applications.

My Story

I began my journey as a self-taught developer, building websites for local businesses while studying Computer Science. After graduating, I joined a tech startup where I honed my skills in full-stack development.

Over the years, I've worked with various technologies and frameworks, from vanilla JavaScript to modern frameworks like React and Next.js. I've led development teams and mentored junior developers, helping them grow their skills.

Today, I specialize in building performant web applications with a focus on user experience and accessibility. I'm passionate about clean code, performance optimization, and creating intuitive interfaces.
